Historical Places

Distric Six

District Six got its name from having been the Sixth Municipal District of Cape Town in 1867. Its earlier unofficial name was Kanaldorp.Also it is an old Methodist Mission Church.

City Hall

Cape Town City Hall is a large Edwardian building in Cape Town city center. It was built in 1905 and is built from honey-colored limestone impoertes from England. It is located on the Grand Parade to the west of the Castle.

Castle of GoodHope

The Castle of GoodHope is well known as the central landmark to the history of Cape Town. It was built in the 7th century.Now the castle is headquarter of the South African military in the Western Cape province.
